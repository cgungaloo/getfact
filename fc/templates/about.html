Welcome to Get Fact

What is it?

This is a place where you can find out if an alleged fact has any credible sources behind it

Alot out there is presented as fact and it can be difficult to find a sources to support them. Lets see if we can end that here!

How does it work?

Create and Account

Click on "New Fact Check"

Post the fact that you would like to get a citation on, for example: "Is it true that the President said ....."

Keep your post concise. It also helps to have one fact check per post as it makes it easier to have your question answered

Sit back and allow others to provide citations for your post

Get Fact allows others to rate the truthfulness of a citiation to help you prioritise the best sources out there

Have fun

Why do I need this?

You need this if you are trying to find references to things you have heard. Perhaps you are struggling to find answers through search engines or maybe you need a citiation thats highly specific and requires a community of truth seekers to help you out

Whether you are trying to sift through the noise coming out of the media or you want to make your homework as accurate as possible, Get Fact!

Rules and guidelines

Be excellent to each other! Lets be respectful to each other, this is a place to find the truth so please avoid trolling, abuse, bullying and general rudeness

Avoid essays. This is not your personal blog, avoid long questions as it makes it hard for people to understand what you want as a citation.

Answer with references. Provide credible links to websites/tweets/articles etc and state where you can find the source if its in a large site or block of text. If you provide a reference to an offline source like a book, please try to state as much information as you can i.e. the title, author, edition, chapter and if possible page number along with any quotations.

Like a fact check! If someone is getting a fact check that you think others should know about, like it!

Rate an answer based on its truthfulness. It's not about whether you like it or not, its about if the citation is credible and true!
